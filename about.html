About

I am YANG, Hong-Bin, Taiwanese. Petingo is my nickname as well as my ID online.

Currently, I work as a research assistant @ Academia Sinica, Taiwan, conducting research on deep learning-based image forgery detection under the supervision of Prof. Chen, Jun-Cheng.

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science @ Taiwan Tech, and I have been to Czech Technical University in Prague as an exchange student during the harsh time of COVID-19. My previous research focused on image/video compression and quality enhancement. During university, I collaborated with Prof. Kuo-Liang Chung and published Effective Chroma Subsampling and Luma Modification for RGB Full-color Images Using the Multiple Linear Regression Technique, which is now state-of-the-art in YUV420 chroma subsampling.

I am also an award-winning photographer, where I won the Special Award in 2021 Shin Kong Mitsukoshi International Photography Contest with this photo. If you are interested in my photography works and want to see more, please visit my vsco or follow my instagram.

Another interest of mine is architecture. Currently, I am working on some projects related to computer aided architectural design and interactive space. I am also preparing for pursuing a Master related to Human-computer interaction, interactive design, and architecture.

If you want to contact me, feel free to PM me through instagram or email: eyanghongbin0315@gmail.com.
